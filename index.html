Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions from foreign investors.

How long does it take to register a WFOE in China?

Typically, the process takes 2-3 months from document preparation to receiving the business license, though this can vary by city and industry.

Do I need to be physically present in China for the setup?

No. Most of the process can be handled remotely. We can represent you with a Power of Attorney. Some steps, like opening a corporate bank account, may require the legal representative to be present.

What is the minimum registered capital required?

While there's officially no minimum for most industries, the amount should be sufficient to cover your business operations for at least the first year. We provide guidance based on your business plan.
